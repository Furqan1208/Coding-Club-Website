Welcome to Code Hawks Club

We are a student-led group known as Code Hawks Club dedicated to promoting coding culture at NED University of Engineering and Technology. Our mission is to provide a platform for students to enhance their coding skills, learn new technologies, and collaborate with their peers.

Our Sponsor

OctDaily Pvt. Ltd empowers businesses and individuals with innovative technology solutions to help them achieve their goals.

On January 26th, 2023, OctDaily Pvt. Ltd. gifted a state-of-the-art Computer Lab to NEDUET. The lab has a capacity of 50 students and was inaugurated by OctDaily's CEO, Ms. Saira Ahmed, and NEDUET's Vice Chancellor, Dr. Sarosh Hasmat Lodhi. The initiative was highly appreciated by Dr. Lodhi as it strengthens the industry-academia collaboration and is expected to benefit students and faculty members.

This Computer Lab is going to play a vital role in the training and enhancement of technical skills of the undergraduate and postgraduate students as well as benefit faculty members and researchers of the department.

Thank you OctDaily Pvt. Ltd. for your generous support!

Our Aims and Objectives

  • Facilitating learning and sharing knowledge about programming languages and related technologies
  • Providing opportunities for students to work on projects and enhance their skills
  • Promoting innovation and creativity in the field of computer science

Our History

The NEDUET Coding Club was founded in 2015 by a group of passionate students who wanted to create a space where they could learn and share their knowledge about programming. Over the years, the club has grown in membership and scope, organizing workshops, coding competitions, and other events that promote coding culture at NEDUET.

Some notable achievements of the club include:

  • Winning the first prize in the National Coding Competition in 2017
  • Organizing the first-ever Hackathon at NEDUET in 2018
  • Partnering with Google for the Google Developer Student Clubs program in 2019
