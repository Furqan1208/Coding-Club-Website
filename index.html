The coding club is a student-run organization focused on helping members develop their coding skills and pursue their interests in technology through various activities and events.

What programming languages does the coding club focus on?

The coding club is open to exploring a wide range of programming languages, including but not limited to Python, Java, JavaScript, C++, and HTML/CSS.

To get involved with the coding club, simply attend one of our meetings or events, or reach out to one of our club officers or members. You can also follow us on social media to stay up-to-date on our latest news and events.
