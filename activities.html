Upcoming Events

Code Jam

Code Jam is an annual programming competition organized by Google. The competition consists of several rounds where participants solve challenging algorithmic problems in a limited amount of time. The competition is open to all and has a huge community of participants from around the world. The top performers from each round move on to the final round, where they compete for the grand prize of $15,000.

Topcoder

Topcoder is a leading competitive programming platform that offers several coding competitions throughout the year. The platform hosts a wide range of competitions, including algorithmic challenges, code sprints, and design contests. Topcoder has a large community of participants from around the world, making it an excellent platform for networking and building relationships with other programmers.

4 Days left

ICPC

The ICPC is an annual programming competition that brings together the best programmers from universities around the world. The competition consists of several rounds, and each round has multiple problems that participants must solve in a limited amount of time. The competition is highly competitive and offers participants the opportunity to showcase their programming skills on a global stage. The top performers from each round move on to the final round.

5 Days left

Previous Events

CodeCon

CodeCon is a coding competition that aims to help programmers hone their skills and compete against the best in the region. The competition consists of a series of challenges, each with a set of problems that participants must solve in a limited amount of time. The competition is open to all students and offers prizes to the top performers.

Hackathon

Hackathon 2022 was a high-energy competition where students pushed their coding skills to the limit. The event was held on March 25, 2022 at the university's computer science building. There were over 100 students who participated in the event, and they were divided into teams of 4. Each team had 24 hours to create a working prototype of a new software application.
